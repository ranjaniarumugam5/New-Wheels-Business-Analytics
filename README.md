# New Wheels Business Analytics

## Project Overview

New Wheels is a vehicle resale company experiencing declining revenue, reduced order volume, and worsening customer feedback. The objective of this project was to analyze business performance using SQL and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

## Business Objective

Develop quarterly business reports for the CEO by analyzing customer behavior, sales performance, revenue trends, feedback ratings, and shipping operations.

## Tools & Skills Used

* SQL
* Joins
* GROUP BY
* CASE WHEN
* Subqueries
* Window Functions (RANK, LAG)
* Business Analytics
* Data Analysis

## Key Business Insights

### Customer Analysis

* Identified 994 unique customers across multiple states.
* California and Florida showed the highest customer concentration.

### Customer Satisfaction Analysis

* Average customer rating declined from Q1 to Q4.
* Negative feedback increased significantly over time.
* Customer satisfaction deteriorated across all quarters.

### Revenue & Order Analysis

* Revenue declined from approximately 18M in Q1 to 8.5M in Q4.
* Orders dropped from 310 to 199 across the same period.
* Strong correlation observed between declining orders and revenue.

### Operational Analysis

* Shipping time increased drastically across quarters.
* Delivery delays emerged as the primary cause of customer dissatisfaction.
* Poor customer experience contributed to lower orders and revenue.

## Business Recommendations

* Improve logistics and delivery operations.
* Reduce shipping delays through supply chain optimization.
* Implement real-time order tracking.
* Improve customer support and communication.
* Monitor customer feedback regularly to improve retention.

## Project Outcome

The analysis revealed that increasing shipping delays were the primary driver behind declining customer satisfaction, reduced order volume, and falling revenue, enabling data-driven recommendations for business improvement.
